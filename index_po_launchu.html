PRO tips

It’s NOT the camera who takes photos, it’s YOU. So here’s a few tricks for you to take the best stillgrams ever.

  • PRO TIP 1 / 3

    Aim for moving crowds

    You want to look for an angle from which the crowds are moving. Stillgram is measuring the crowd's density every second. When it’s to high, we’ll ask you to look for a better shot for the A.I. to feel relaxed working on it.

  • PRO TIP 2 / 3

    Avoid static crowds

    Stillgram’s A.I. needs a glimpse of what’s behind the people. Otherwise it’s not able to make all the right bets. So avoid crowds standing dead still or moving towards you.
